Here's a comprehensive list of things you'll need to run a business:

Planning and Strategy
1. *Business plan*: Outline your business goals, target market, financial projections, and marketing strategy.
2. *Mission statement*: Define your business's purpose, values, and objectives.
3. *Vision statement*: Describe your business's long-term goals and aspirations.

Registration and Licenses
1. *Business registration*: Register your business with the relevant government agencies.
2. *Business license*: Obtain necessary licenses and permits to operate your business.
3. *Tax registration*: Register for taxes, such as income tax, sales tax, and employment tax.

Financial Management
1. *Accounting system*: Set up an accounting system to track income, expenses, and financial performance.
2. *Bank account*: Open a business bank account to separate personal and business finances.
3. *Payment processing*: Set up payment processing systems, such as credit card processing or online payment gateways.

Marketing and Sales
1. *Marketing strategy*: Develop a marketing strategy to reach your target audience.
2. *Website*: Create a professional website to showcase your products or services.
3. *Social media presence*: Establish a presence on relevant social media platforms.

Operations and Management
1. *Business location*: Secure a physical or virtual location for your business.
2. *Equipment and supplies*: Acquire necessary equipment and supplies to operate your business.
3. *Employee management*: Develop policies and procedures for managing employees, if applicable.

Risk Management
1. *Insurance*: Obtain necessary insurance coverage, such as liability insurance or business interruption insurance.
2. *Compliance*: Ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations.
3. *Data security*: Implement measures to protect sensitive business data.

Technology and Infrastructure
1. *Computer systems*: Set up computer systems, including hardware and software.
2. *Internet and phone*: Establish internet and phone connectivity.
3. *Software applications*: Implement necessary software applications, such as accounting software or customer relationship management (CRM) software.

Human Resources
1. *Employee handbook*: Develop an employee handbook outlining policies and procedures.
2. *Training and development*: Provide training and development opportunities for employees.
3. *Benefits and compensation*: Offer competitive benefits and compensation packages.

Ongoing Operations
1. *Performance monitoring*: Regularly monitor business performance, including financials and customer satisfaction.
2. *Continuous improvement*: Continuously evaluate and improve business operations, products, and services.
3. *Adaptability*: Stay adaptable and responsive to changes in the market, industry, or economy.
